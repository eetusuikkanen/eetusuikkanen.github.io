Eetu Suikkanen is a versatile composer from Finland, who crafts music across a wide range of styles. Eetu's work is characterized by its emotional depth and blending of both traditional and modern elements, making his compositions resonate with a diverse audience.

He continually explores new genres and techniques, pushing the boundaries of his craft. Wheter working on piano pieces, electronic music, or experimental sounds, Eetu's dedication to his art is evident in every note.
