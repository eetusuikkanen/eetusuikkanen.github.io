# What is this?
This is a little rpg maker game I started for fun a while back, intending to continue the material i worked on for the Impressions Composing jam: Mystery #1 jam event, where I made a little RPG demo game for the submission, using RPGMAKER VX Ace, but bought the newer RPGMAKER MV later that can make web builds to play from the browser.

Still just the old jam music on a little generic Final Fantasy "GATHER THE CRYSTALS HEROES!!" setting, but when I get around to it/feel like it, I will try to make a full gameplay loop, even if a short one, with some new music to go with it..

Initial idea is maybe do some SNES style stuff, but the free sprite assets I found for this are pretty 8bit, and the original jam stuff was too, so might just keep it on the NES sounds (did make 1 new SNES sound track for the intro dialog as a test)
